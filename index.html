About Me

Hey there, I'm Scott Cheung and I'm excited to share a little about myself with you. As a web developer, my goal is to create digital experiences that are technically sound and user friendly. I enjoy using an ever-growing tech stack to develop visually stunning websites that are accessible and responsive for everyone.

Thanks for getting to know me!
